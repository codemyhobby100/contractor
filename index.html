About Us

Who We Are

We started A and I Nigeria Enterprises back in July 1999 with a simple dream: to be the best place for building supplies and friendly service in Nigeria. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted partner for countless clients, and that's something we're incredibly proud of. Think of us as your one-stop shop for all things building and general merchandise. Need iron rods, tiles, or even plumbing essentials? We've got a vast selection to choose from. But we don't stop there! Building a house? Our team of experts offers construction services like block manufacturing, bricklaying, and even water drainage systems. We can help you with every step of the project. Quality is what matters most to us. That's why we source and import top-notch materials, so you can be sure you're getting the best for your project. Your satisfaction is our priority. We're here to answer your questions, offer advice, and make sure you have everything you need to get the job done right.
